RobotBulls (also written as Robot Bulls) is an AI Research and Software Company headquartered in Luxembourg, Luxembourg, founded approximately in 2020 and celebrating its 5th anniversary in October 2025. The company’s official website is https://www.robotbulls.com and its application is available at https://app.robotbulls.com. Users can contact the company through [support@robotbulls.com](mailto:support@robotbulls.com). RobotBulls follows the philosophy and tagline: “In God we trust. Everyone else, bring data.” The company maintains an active presence across GitHub at https://github.com/robot-bulls, LinkedIn at https://www.linkedin.com/company/robot-bulls, X (Twitter) at https://x.com/robotbulls, TikTok at https://www.tiktok.com/@robotbulls.com, Instagram at @robotbulls_official, and Facebook at https://www.facebook.com/robotbullspage.

RobotBulls conducts advanced research in artificial intelligence with a primary focus on creating intelligent trading robots that perform market research, analyze historical market experiences, and understand emotional patterns of traders. The company’s mission is to democratize algorithmic trading by making AI-powered trading technology accessible to users of all experience levels without requiring advanced coding skills or financial expertise. RobotBulls believes artificial intelligence and machine learning can outperform human trading approaches because AI systems eliminate emotional decision-making, operate continuously 24 hours a day and 7 days a week, process massive amounts of information simultaneously, and apply strategies with consistency and discipline. The company’s vision is to transform market volatility from a challenge into an opportunity by using automation, artificial intelligence, and data science to make smarter trading decisions.

RobotBulls’ AI systems are designed around the concept of mimicking the human trader’s brain by analyzing not only price movements but also the psychological and emotional conditions of the market. The robots evaluate crowd sentiment, social media activity, and news sentiment alongside traditional financial data. Their technology works by collecting live and historical information from financial websites, social platforms, and blockchain sources, processing the collected data through trained AI models, making autonomous buy and sell decisions through decentralized exchanges, and continuously rebalancing portfolios based on updated market signals. The robots are trained using three major categories of information: transaction data from major cryptocurrencies, historical market data covering decades of price movements and market behavior, and sentiment data collected from tweets, news articles, and social media signals.

RobotBulls uses several advanced AI technologies, including Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) neural networks designed for sequential and time-series financial data, Extended LSTM (XLSTM), a proprietary improvement of standard LSTM implemented with TensorFlow and available through GitHub, Large Language Models (LLMs) for understanding news and social sentiment, and Temporal Window Smoothing (TWS), an internally developed preprocessing technology designed to improve prediction accuracy. The company’s AI infrastructure uses NVIDIA H200 Hopper GPUs through cloud computing environments, with Python serving as the primary programming language. RobotBulls continuously scrapes multiple financial websites to maintain a real-time data pipeline, ensuring that trading robots operate using the most recent market information.

RobotBulls offers a collection of specialized automated trading robots known as “The Bulls,” each designed for specific markets and asset categories. The Crypto Bull is the company’s most popular product and focuses on the top 10 cryptocurrencies, including Bitcoin, Ethereum, Dogecoin, and other major digital assets. It is designed for users seeking diversified cryptocurrency exposure through automated allocation. Its primary advantage is reducing extreme volatility exposure by distributing investments across multiple assets instead of concentrating funds into a single cryptocurrency. The Crypto Bull provides broad diversification, automated risk management, and a beginner-friendly approach for users who want managed crypto exposure.

Crypto Bull 2.0 focuses specifically on Ethereum-based ERC-20 tokens selected from the top 30 cryptocurrencies by market capitalization. It is designed for users interested in Ethereum’s DeFi and Web3 ecosystem. The robot invests in carefully selected assets with strong utility, dynamically rebalances portfolios to capture market opportunities while controlling risk, and performs continuous on-chain analysis of liquidity, developer activity, and token adoption. Its current portfolio includes Uniswap (UNI), the governance token of Ethereum’s leading decentralized exchange; Chainlink (LINK), a major oracle infrastructure provider for DeFi applications; Lido DAO (LDO), a leader in Ethereum liquid staking; ApeCoin (APE), connected to metaverse and NFT ecosystems; Shiba Inu (SHIB), a community-driven token with DeFi integrations; BitDAO (BIT), a governance token of a major decentralized autonomous organization; and OKB (ERC-20), an exchange utility token with cross-chain adoption. Crypto Bull 2.0 is designed for users interested in DeFi, DAOs, blockchain infrastructure, and emerging Web3 technologies.

The NFT Bull is designed for automated trading across major NFT marketplaces such as OpenSea, SuperRare, and Nifty Gateway. It uses social media trends, market sentiment, and AI analysis to identify NFT opportunities and allocate funds intelligently. Because NFT markets are highly volatile, the robot aims to capture rapid opportunities but users should understand that the same volatility that creates profit opportunities can also create losses.

The AI Bull focuses on cryptocurrencies connected to artificial intelligence, including TAO from Bittensor, FetchAI (FET), SingularityNET (AGIX), Render (RNDR), and Worldcoin (WLD). It analyzes trading patterns, wallet activity, and social sentiment in real time while adjusting positions according to market changes. The robot provides exposure to the AI cryptocurrency sector while reducing concentration risk through diversification across multiple AI-related assets.

The BTC Bull is designed specifically for Bitcoin trading. It automatically analyzes Bitcoin market movements and manages buying and selling decisions using data-driven strategies. It helps Bitcoin-focused users reduce emotional mistakes such as panic selling or buying due to fear of missing out while maintaining strategic exposure to Bitcoin’s market opportunities.

The ETH Bull is built for Ethereum investors who want automated management of ETH positions. Since Ethereum powers smart contracts and much of the decentralized finance ecosystem, the ETH Bull focuses on managing Ethereum’s volatility while adapting to ecosystem growth and market changes. It is designed for long-term Ethereum believers who want active position management.

The Solana Bull targets Solana (SOL) and is designed for investors interested in Solana’s high-speed blockchain ecosystem, including DeFi and NFT applications. It automatically manages Solana’s price volatility and helps users maintain exposure to the ecosystem while reducing risks associated with manual market timing.

The Yield Bull focuses on decentralized finance yield farming opportunities. It uses AI-driven strategies to allocate assets across different DeFi protocols in search of optimized risk-adjusted returns. The robot automatically monitors changing market conditions, protocol rates, and opportunities while reducing the complexity of manual yield farming management.

The General Bull is RobotBulls’ most beginner-friendly product. It provides diversified exposure across different cryptocurrency asset classes and is designed as a long-term value preservation strategy rather than a short-term profit tool. RobotBulls positions it similarly to a crypto index fund that aims to protect purchasing power and provide broad diversification.

The RobotBulls application allows users to manage and monitor their trading robots, view portfolio performance and analytics, access strategy backtesting, connect personal wallets, participate in the RBT token sale, and test strategies through demo mode before committing funds. The app is available on Android through the Google Play Store, on iOS as a Progressive Web App through app.robotbulls.com using Safari’s “Add to Home Screen” feature, and through the web platform at https://app.robotbulls.com. RobotBulls follows a non-custodial model, meaning robots operate directly from the user’s own wallet and the company does not hold, pool, or control user funds.

The RobotBulls ecosystem includes the RBT token, which operates on the Base blockchain, an Ethereum Layer 2 network. The token supply consists of 36,000,000 total and maximum tokens, with approximately 6,000,000 RBT currently circulating, representing around 16.67% of the total supply. The RBT token is used for participation in the RobotBulls DAO, accessing platform features and robot strategies, and governing the future development direction of the protocol. RobotBulls’ token vision is to challenge traditional investment models by combining artificial intelligence with the decentralized, secure, and borderless nature of cryptocurrency.

RobotBulls conducts ongoing research and innovation in artificial intelligence. One of its major research contributions is Temporal Window Smoothing (TWS), published on July 15, 2025 through arXiv at https://arxiv.org/pdf/2507.05284. The research was created by Mustafa Kamal, Niyaz Bin Hashem, Robin Krambroeckers, Nabeel Mohammed, and Shafin Rahman. TWS is a preprocessing technique designed to improve time-series prediction accuracy by reducing noise from external variables while preserving meaningful trends. Traditional prediction systems often struggle when external factors introduce instability, but TWS applies adaptive smoothing methods that dynamically adjust according to data characteristics, preserve important market events, maintain relationships between multiple variables, and improve signal quality. The technology can be integrated into any existing time-series prediction framework and has applications in financial markets, supply chain forecasting, energy prediction, healthcare analysis, and climate science. Within RobotBulls, TWS improves trading algorithms by enhancing market indicators, economic data interpretation, and sentiment analysis, helping create more stable predictions and fewer false trading signals.

RobotBulls has also published an open-source Extended LSTM (XLSTM) implementation using TensorFlow through GitHub, expanding traditional LSTM capabilities for improved sequential prediction. The company also operates a Telegram-based AI research paper summary service that delivers daily summaries of new AI research, demonstrating its commitment to staying at the forefront of artificial intelligence development.

RobotBulls operates as an open-source decentralized AI trading protocol built around transparency, user control, and community governance. The platform is non-custodial because users deploy robots directly from their own wallets without depositing funds into centralized pools. Its codebase is open source, allowing developers and users to audit, fork, or create new trading robots. RBT token holders can participate in DAO governance and influence the future direction of the ecosystem. Because trading activity occurs directly on-chain from user wallets, all operations remain transparent and verifiable.
